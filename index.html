Rice grain quality book

I'm a contributing author to "Rice Grain Quality: Methods and Protocols", a newly published book featuring non-routine methods to evaluate rice quality and how data science approaches could be used to find new insights about rice quality.

If you're interested in how rice, one of the world's most important staples, is evaluated, check out the book!
How much does it cost to stay in SF?

San Francisco is one of the most visited cities in the world. It's pretty expensive to stay in it too. This study explores the different accommodations rates in the City's neighbourhoods.

After this study, I learned that SF's borders go beyond the downtown area and the financial district.
