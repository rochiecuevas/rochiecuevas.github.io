About Rochie GitHub Twitter LinkedIn

I am a data scientist formally trained as a food scientist. I previously worked at the International Rice Research Institute, unraveling starch structural properties, tasting rice, and using data science to help understand rice quality needs of consumers and how rice varietal improvement programs could deliver improved quality to rice consumers.

Currently, I am studying data analytics and visualisation at the University of California, Berkeley. I earned a Ph.D. in Agricultural Science from the University of Queensland, researching the structural properties of glutinous rice. I earned a B.Sc. from the University of the Philippines Los Baños, where I majored in microbiology.
